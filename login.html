Game Directions

Game Rules

In the game room, you'll be shown a random photo.

Your job is to use as many words as you can to describe what you see. You will only have ONE MINUTE to accomplish this task.

Earning Points

To earn a point, your answers must match the person you are playing with.

For each matching word, you both gain a point.

Earning Bonus Points

For each matching word that is in our hidden bonus word list, you both gain 2 points.
