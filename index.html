Game Directions

About the Game

  • When you enter the game room, you will be paired with another player. Each of you, typing one word at at time, is to describe what you see in detail. If you type matching words, you both gain a point. You will only have ONE MINUTE to accomplish this task.

How to Play

  • Sign-in using google login.
  • Click the puppy image to start game.
  • A one minute timer witll start.
  • Enter descriptive term into word box.
  • Press submit or enter after each word.
  • After timer expires, click bird button to restart
  • A point is earned with both people use the same word.

Bonus Points

  • For each matching word that is in our hidden bonus word list, you both gain 2 points.
